Important Notice

MyBella AI companions are designed to provide emotional support and companionship, but they are not a substitute for professional mental health care. If you are experiencing thoughts of self-harm or suicide, please contact emergency services or one of the crisis hotlines listed above immediately.

Remember: It's okay to not be okay. Help is available, and you don't have to face difficult times alone.
